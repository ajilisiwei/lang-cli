### can /kən/ modal verb 能够
She can cook Italian food like a chef. ->> 她能像厨师一样做意大利菜。
Can you drive a manual car? ->> 你能开手动挡的车吗？

### boss /bɔs/ n. 老板，上司
My boss praised my hard work at the meeting. ->> 我的老板在会议上表扬了我的努力工作。
He has been working with the same boss for five years. ->> 他已经和同一位老板共事五年了。

### minute /ˈmɪnət/ n. 分（钟）
She waited for ten minutes before the bus arrived. ->> 她等了十分钟才等到公共汽车。
Could you hold on for just a minute? ->> 你能稍等一分钟吗？

### ask /æsk/ v. 请求，要求
Don't hesitate to ask for help if you need it. ->> 如果你需要帮助，不要犹豫去请求。
She asked him to pick up some groceries on his way home. ->> 她让他回家的路上买些杂货。

### handwriting /ˈhænˌdraɪtɪŋ/ n. 书写
His handwriting is difficult to decipher. ->> 他的书写很难辨认。
The teacher commented on the neatness of her handwriting. ->> 老师称赞了她书写的整齐。

### terrible /ˈtɛrəbəl/ adj. 糟糕的，可怕的
I had a terrible headache this morning. ->> 我今天早上头疼得厉害。
They faced a terrible storm during their journey. ->> 他们在旅途中遭遇了暴风雨。
