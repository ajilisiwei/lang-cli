whose /huz/ pron. 谁的  
Whose keys are on the counter? ->> 柜台上的钥匙是谁的？  
Whose dog is barking outside? ->> 在外面吠叫的狗是谁的？

blue /blu/ adj. 蓝色的  
He painted the wall a calming shade of blue. ->> 他把墙刷成了一种令人平静的蓝色。  
Her eyes were a striking blue, like the ocean. ->> 她的眼睛是惊人的蓝色，像大海一样。

perhaps /pərˈhæps/ adv. 大概  
Perhaps it will rain later, so take an umbrella. ->> 也许待会会下雨，所以带把伞吧。  
She's perhaps studying in the library right now. ->> 她大概现在在图书馆学习。

white /waɪt/ adj. 白色的  
The white sand on the beach was soft and warm. ->> 海滩上的白沙柔软而温暖。  
He always preferred white shirts for formal events. ->> 他总是更喜欢在正式场合穿白衬衫。

catch /kæʧ/ v. 抓住  
Did you catch what he said during the meeting? ->> 你听清楚他在会议上说的话了吗？  
She ran fast enough to catch the train. ->> 她跑得很快，赶上了火车。
