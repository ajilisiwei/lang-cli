Could you spell the address out for me? ->> 你能帮我拼一下这个地址吗？  
Children often learn to spell by using phonics exercises. ->> 孩子们通常通过语音练习学习拼写。

Her intelligent remarks impressed everyone at the meeting. ->> 她聪明的发言让会议上的每个人都印象深刻。

She realized her mistake and quickly corrected it. ->> 她意识到了自己的错误并迅速改正。  
It's okay to make a mistake as long as you learn from it. ->> 只要从中学习，犯错误是可以接受的。
