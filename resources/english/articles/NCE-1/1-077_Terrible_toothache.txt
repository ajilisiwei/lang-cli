appointment /əˈpɔɪntmənt/ n. 约会，预约  
I have a haircut appointment at noon. ->> 我中午有个理发的预约。  
He scheduled an appointment with his financial advisor. ->> 他预约了和他的财务顾问见面。

urgent /ˈɜːrdʒənt/ adj. 紧急的，急迫的  
The doctor was called for an urgent case at the hospital. ->> 医生被叫去医院处理一件紧急情况。  
We need an urgent response to this crisis. ->> 我们需要对这一危机作出紧急响应。

till /tɪl/ prep. 直到……为止  
I'll wait for you till the last train leaves. ->> 我会等你到最后一班火车离开。  
Keep the door open till the guests arrive. ->> 把门开着直到客人们到来。
