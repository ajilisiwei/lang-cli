water /ˈwɔtər/ v. 浇水  
The gardener waters the lawn twice a week. ->> 园丁每周给草坪浇水两次。  
Don't forget to water the vegetables in the greenhouse. ->> 别忘了给温室里的蔬菜浇水。  

terribly /ˈtɛrəbli/ adv. 非常  
She was terribly disappointed with the results. ->> 她对结果非常失望。  
The weather was terribly cold last night. ->> 昨晚天气非常冷。  

dry /draɪ/ adj. 干燥的，干的  
After hours in the sun, the ground became dry. ->> 在阳光下几个小时后，地面变得干燥了。  
This cake is a bit dry, don't you think? ->> 你不觉得这个蛋糕有点干吗？  

nuisance /ˈnusəns/ n. 讨厌的东西或人  
Traffic can be a nuisance during the rush hour. ->> 在高峰时段，交通可能是个麻烦。  
Barking dogs can be a nuisance to the neighbors. ->> 吠叫的狗可能对邻居造成困扰。  

mean (meant, meant) /min/ v. 意味着，意思是  
Does his silence mean he's angry? ->> 他的沉默是否意味着他生气了？  
This symbol means 'no entry' in most countries. ->> 这个符号在大多数国家意味着“禁止入内”。  

surprise /səˈpraɪz/ n. 惊奇，意外的事
