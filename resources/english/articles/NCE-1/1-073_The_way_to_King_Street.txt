Last week Mrs Mills went to London. ->> 上星期米尔斯夫人去了伦敦。
She does not know London very well, and she lost her way. ->> 她对伦敦不很熟悉，因此迷了路。
Suddenly, she saw a man near a bus-stop. “ I can ask him the way.” She said to herself. ->> 突然，她在公共汽车站附近看到一个男人。“我可以向他问路，”她想。
“Excuse me,” she said. ->> “对不起，”她说。
“Can you tell me the way to King Street please?” ->> “您能告诉我到国王街怎么走吗？”
The man smiled pleasantly. ->> 这人友好地笑了笑。
He did not understand English! ->> 他不懂英语！
He spoke German. ->> 他讲德语。
He was a tourist. ->> 他是个旅游者。
Then he put his hand into his pocket, and took out a phrase-book. ->> 然后他把手伸进衣袋，掏出了一本常用语手册。
He opened the book and found a phrase. ->> 他翻开书找到了一条短语。
He read the phrase slowly. ->> 他缓慢地读着短语。
“I am sorry,” he said. “ I do not speak English.” ->> “很抱歉，”他说，“我不会讲英语。”
