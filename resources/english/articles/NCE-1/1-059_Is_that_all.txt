LADY: I want some envelopes please. ->> 女士：请给我拿几个信封。  
STATIONER: Do you want the large size, or the small size? ->> 售货员：您要大号的还是小号的？  
LADY: The large size please. ->> 女士：请拿大号的。  
LADY: Do you have any writing-paper? ->> 女士：您有信纸吗？  
Stationer: Yes, we do. ->> 售货员：有。  
Stationer: I don't have any small pads. ->> 售货员：我没有小本的信纸。  
I only have large ones. ->> 售货员：我只有大本的。  
Do you want a pad? ->> 售货员：您要一本吗？  
LADY: Yes, please. ->> 女士：好的，请拿一本。  
LADY: And I want some black ink and some glue. ->> 女士：我还要一些黑色墨水和胶水。  
Stationer: A bottle of ink and a bottle of glue. ->> 售货员：一瓶墨水和一瓶胶水。  
LADY: And I want a large box of chalk, too. ->> 女士：我还要一大盒粉笔。  
Stationer: I only have small boxes. ->> 售货员：我只有小盒的。  
Do you want one? ->> 售货员：您要一盒吗？  
LADY: No, thank you. ->> 女士：不了，谢谢。  
Stationer: Is that all? ->> 售货员：就这些吗？  
LADY: That's all, thank you. ->> 女士：就这些，谢谢。  
Stationer: What else do you want? ->> 售货员：您还要别的吗？  
LADY: I want my change. ->> 女士：请找给我零钱。

She doodled on the pad during the meeting. ->> 她在会议期间在信纸簿上涂鸦。

I accidentally spilled glue on the table. ->> 我不小心把胶水洒在桌子上了。  
They used glue to assemble the model airplane. ->> 他们用胶水拼装飞机模型。

She counted her change to make sure it was correct. ->> 她数了数她的零钱以确保无误。
