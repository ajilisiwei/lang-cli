colour /ˈkələr/ n. 颜色  
The autumn leaves change colour to a beautiful orange. ->> 秋天的叶子变成了美丽的橙色。  
My favourite colour is blue because it reminds me of the ocean. ->> 我最喜欢的颜色是蓝色，因为它让我想起海洋。  

green /grin/ adj. 绿色  
The traffic light turned green, and we started to drive. ->> 红绿灯变绿后，我们开始开车。  
Her garden is filled with green plants and flowers. ->> 她的花园里满是绿色的植物和花朵。  

come /kəm/ v. 来  
She promised to come and see us during the holidays. ->> 她答应在假期来看看我们。  
When the rain stopped, he decided to come outside. ->> 雨停后，他决定出来。  

upstairs /əpˈstɛrz/ adv. 楼上  
I left my keys upstairs in my bedroom. ->> 我把钥匙忘在了楼上的卧室里。  
The kids ran upstairs to play in their rooms. ->> 孩子们跑到楼上他们的房间里玩。  

smart /smɑrt/ adj. 时髦的，巧妙的  
She wore a smart dress to the interview, making a great impression. ->> 她穿了一件时髦的连衣裙去面试，给人留下了深刻的印象。  
His solution to the problem was very smart and effective. ->> 他对问题的解决方案既巧妙又有效。  

hat /hæt/ n. 帽子  
During the cold winter, he always wears a warm hat. ->> 在寒冷的冬天，他总是戴着一顶温暖的帽子。  
She made a hat from wool as a gift for her friend. ->> 她用羊毛做了一顶帽子送给朋友。  

same /seɪm/ adj. 相同的  
They both reached the same conclusion independently. ->> 他们两人独立得出了相同的结论。  
We arrived at the same time, despite taking different routes. ->> 尽管我们选了不同的路线，但还是同时到达。  

lovely /ˈləvli/ adj. 可爱的，秀丽的  
The lovely cottage is surrounded by rolling hills. ->> 那个秀丽的小屋被连绵起伏的山丘所环绕。  
Her lovely voice captivated everyone in the room. ->> 她可爱的声音吸引了房间里的每一个人。
