shut /ʃət/ v. 关门  
Please shut the window; it's getting cold. ->> 请关上窗户，天气变冷了。  
He shut his laptop and headed to bed. ->> 他合上笔记本电脑去睡觉了。  

bedroom /ˈbɛˌdrum/ n. 卧室  
The bedroom has a beautiful view of the garden. ->> 卧室可以看到美丽的花园景色。  
She keeps her plants in the bedroom for better air quality. ->> 她把植物放在卧室里以改善空气质量。  

untidy /ənˈtaɪdi/ adj. 乱，不整齐  
My brother's untidy handwriting is hard to read. ->> 我兄弟的字迹不整齐，很难读懂。  
The kitchen became untidy after cooking the large meal. ->> 大餐过后厨房变得很乱。  

must /məst/ modal verb 必须，应该  
You must wear a helmet while riding a bike. ->> 骑自行车时你必须戴头盔。  
We must feed the cat before leaving the house. ->> 我们必须在出门前给猫喂食。  

open /ˈoʊpən/ v. 打开  
He opened the envelope to read the letter. ->> 他打开信封阅读里面的信。  
Could you open that jar for me? It's too tight. ->> 你能帮我打开那个罐子吗？太紧了。  

air /ɛr/ v. 使 … 通风，换换空气  
She airs her running shoes outside to keep them fresh. ->> 她把跑鞋放在外面通风以保持清新。  
We should air the carpet to get rid of the smell. ->> 我们应该晾一下地毯去除异味。  

put /pʊt/ v. 放置  
He put on his jacket before heading out. ->> 他在出门前穿上了夹克。  
Please put your phone on silent during the meeting. ->> 请在会议期间将手机调至静音。  

clothes /kloʊðz/ n. 衣服  
He donated his old clothes to charity. ->> 他把旧衣服捐给了慈善机构。  
She folded the clothes neatly after they dried. ->> 衣服晾干后她把它们整齐地叠好。  

wardrobe /ˈwɔrˌdroʊb/ n. 大衣柜  
She organized the wardrobe by color. ->> 她按颜色整理了大衣柜。  
A mirror is attached to the inside of the wardrobe door. ->> 大衣柜门的内侧装了一面镜子。  

dust /dəst/ v. 掸掉灰尘土  
He dusted the lampshades while cleaning the house. ->> 他在打扫房子时掸掉了灯罩上的灰尘。  
I usually dust the piano once a week. ->> 我通常每周掸掉钢琴上的灰尘一次。  

sweep /swip/ v. 扫  
Don't forget to sweep the porch; it looks messy. ->> 别忘了清扫门廊，看起来很乱。  
After playing outside, she swept the dirt from her shoes. ->> 在外面玩耍后，她扫掉了鞋上的泥土。
