one /wən/ pron. 一个  
There is only one cookie left in the jar. ->> 罐子里只剩下一个饼干了。  
One of my friends is visiting this weekend. ->> 我的一位朋友这个周末来访。

Which book are you reading right now? ->> 你现在在读哪本书？
