excuse /ɪkˈskjuz/ v. 原谅  
Please excuse my tardiness; the traffic was terrible. ->> 请原谅我迟到，交通太糟糕了。  
He excused her late arrival as he knew the circumstances. ->> 他因为知道情况而原谅了她的迟到。

me /mi/ pron. 我(宾格)  
He asked me to join him for dinner. ->> 他邀请我一起吃晚饭。  
Can you show me how this works? ->> 你能教我这个怎么用吗？

yes /jɛs/ adv. 是的  
"Yes, you were right," she admitted. ->> 她承认说：“是的，你是对的。”  
The team cheered "Yes" when they won the match. ->> 队伍赢得比赛时欢呼“是的”。

is /ɪz/ v. be 动词现在时第三人称单数  
The cat is sleeping on the couch. ->> 猫正在沙发上睡觉。  
His accent is quite strong. ->> 他的口音很重。

this /ðɪs/ pron. 这  
This is what I was looking for. ->> 这就是我在找的东西。  
Can you pass me this pen? ->> 你能递给我这个笔吗？

your /jʊr/ possessive adjective 你的，你们的  
Your shoes are by the door. ->> 你的鞋子在门边。  
Is this your jacket on the chair? ->> 椅子上的夹克是你的吗？

handbag /ˈhændˌbæg/ n. （女用）手提包  
She carried her essentials in a small handbag. ->> 她把必需品装在一个小手提包里。  
The lost handbag was returned to its owner. ->> 丢失的手提包被还给了它的主人。

pardon /ˈpɑrdən/ int. 原谅，请再说一遍  
Pardon, could you repeat that? ->> 请再说一遍，你能重复一下吗？  
Pardon me, I didn't catch your name. ->> 请原谅，我没听到你的名字。

it /ɪt/ pron. 它  
It is important to be on time. ->> 准时很重要。  
If it snows, we'll build a snowman. ->> 如果下雪，我们将堆一个雪人。

thank /θæŋk/ you 感谢你（们）  
Thank you for coming to the party. ->> 感谢你来参加聚会。  
Thank you for the delicious meal. ->> 感谢你准备的美味餐点。

very /ˈvɛri/ much 非常地  
We very much regret the inconvenience. ->> 我们非常遗憾带来的不便。  
She very much looks forward to the trip. ->> 她非常期待这次旅行。
