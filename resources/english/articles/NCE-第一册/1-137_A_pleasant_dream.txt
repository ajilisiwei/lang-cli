June: It's a pleasant dream, but everything depends on “if”! ->> 朱莉：这是个美好的梦，但一切都取决于“如果”！

At the casino, they joined a pool to increase their chances of winning. ->> 在赌场，他们参加了赌注以增加获胜的机会。

They were ecstatic when their team won the finals. ->> 他们的队伍赢得决赛时，他们非常高兴。

world /wərld/ n. 世界

The organization aims to support poor communities through education. ->> 该机构旨在通过教育支持贫穷社区。
