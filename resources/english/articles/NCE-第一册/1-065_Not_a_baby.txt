FATHER: What are you going to do this evening, Jill? ->> 父亲：今晚你打算做什么，吉尔？  
Jill: I'm going to meet some friends, Dad. ->> 吉尔：我要去见几个朋友，爸爸。  
FATHER: You mustn't come home late. You must be home at half past ten. ->> 父亲：你不准晚归，必须在十点半到家。  
Jill: I can’t get home so early, Dad. Can I have the key to the front door, please? ->> 吉尔：这么早我赶不回来，爸爸。我能带前门的钥匙吗？  
FATHER: NO, you can't. ->> 父亲：不行，不能带。  
MOTHER: Jill's eighteen years old, Tom. She's not a baby. Give her the key. She always comes home early. ->> 母亲：吉尔已经十八岁了，汤姆。她不是小孩子了。把钥匙给她吧。她总是早早回家的。  
FATHER: Oh, all right! Here you are. But you mustn't come home after a quarter past eleven. Do you hear? ->> 父亲：好吧，拿去。但你不能超过十一点一刻回家。听见了吗？  
Jill: Yes, Dad. Thanks, Mum. ->> 吉尔：听见了，爸爸。谢谢，妈妈。  
MOTHER: That's all right. Goodbye. Enjoy yourself! ->> 母亲：不用谢。再见。玩得开心点！  
Jill: We always enjoy ourselves, Mum. Bye, bye. ->> 吉尔：我们总是玩得很开心，妈妈。再见。


Dad /dæd/ n. 爸（儿语）  
Dad reads me a story every night at bedtime. ->> 爸每天晚上睡前给我讲故事。  
I always look forward to going fishing with Dad. ->> 我总是期待和爸爸一起去钓鱼。

key /ki/ n. 钥匙  
He left his key in the car by accident. ->> 他不小心把钥匙留在了车里。  
Make sure to return the key after you use it. ->> 使用完后一定要归还钥匙。

baby /ˈbeɪbi/ n. 婴儿  
The baby smiled at everyone in the room. ->> 婴儿对房间里的每个人笑。  
She changed the baby's diaper before bed. ->> 她在睡前给婴儿换了尿布。

hear /hir/ v. 听见  
Did you hear the thunder last night? ->> 你昨晚听到雷声了吗？  
I could hear music coming from the party next door. ->> 我能听到隔壁派对传来的音乐声。

enjoy /ɪnˈʤɔɪ/ v. 玩得快活  
She enjoys painting landscapes in her spare time. ->> 她喜欢在业余时间画风景画。  
We enjoy watching movies together during holidays. ->> 我们喜欢在假期一起看电影。

yourself /jɔrˈsɛlf/ pron. 你自己  
Feel free to help yourself to more food. ->> 随意多吃点食物。  
Take a moment for yourself to relax and breathe. ->> 给你自己一点时间放松和呼吸。

ourselves /aʊrˈsɛlvz/ pron. 我们自己  
We decided to paint the room ourselves. ->> 我们决定自己粉刷房间。  
Let's make sure we can rely on ourselves in tough times. ->> 让我们确保在困难时期能够依靠我们自己。

mum /mʌm/ n. 妈妈  
Mum always gives the best advice when I need it. ->> 妈妈总是在我需要时给出最好的建议。  
I surprised mum with a bouquet of flowers today. ->> 我今天用一束花给妈妈一个惊喜。
