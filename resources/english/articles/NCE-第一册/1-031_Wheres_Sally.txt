garden /ˈgɑrdən/ n. 花园  
The children are playing hide and seek in the garden. ->> 孩子们在花园里玩捉迷藏。  
Our garden is full of blooming roses in the spring. ->> 我们的花园里春天开满了玫瑰花。  

under /ˈəndər/ prep. 在……下面  
He hid the letter under his pillow. ->> 他把信藏在枕头下面。  
The cat is hiding under the couch. ->> 猫躲在沙发下面。  

tree /tri/ n. 树  
They planted a cherry tree in the front yard. ->> 他们在前院种了一棵樱桃树。  
A bird built a nest in the tree by the window. ->> 一只鸟在窗边的树上筑了巢。  

climb /klaɪm/ v. 爬，攀登  
The hikers planned to climb the tallest peak in the region. ->> 登山者计划攀登这个地区最高的山峰。  
Watch out when you climb that rock; it can be slippery. ->> 你爬那块石头时要小心，它可能会滑。  

who /hu/ pron. 谁  
Who will be the guest speaker at the conference? ->> 谁会是会议的特邀演讲者？  
Do you know who will attend the meeting from our team? ->> 你知道我们队里谁会参加会议吗？  

run /rən/ v. 跑  
She decided to run a marathon next year. ->> 她决定明年跑一场马拉松。  
The dog loves to run along the beach in the morning. ->> 狗喜欢早上沿着海滩跑。  

grass /græs/ n. 草，草地  
The gardener mowed the grass in the afternoon. ->> 园丁下午修剪了草坪。  
We spread a blanket on the grass for the picnic. ->> 我们在草地上铺了条毯子野餐。  

after /ˈæftər/ prep. 在……之后  
Let's meet at the cafe after work. ->> 我们下班后在咖啡馆碰面吧。  
She felt much better after taking a short nap. ->> 小睡一会儿后她感觉好多了。  

across /əˈkrɔs/ prep. 横过，穿过  
They built a new road across the valley. ->> 他们在山谷上修了一条新路。  
We could see the castle across the lake. ->> 我们可以看到湖对面的城堡。  

cat /kæt/ n. 猫  
My neighbor's cat always visits our garden. ->> 我邻居的猫总是来我们的花园。  
The cat stretched lazily in the sunshine. ->> 猫在阳光下懒洋洋地伸展。
