matter /ˈmætər/ n. 事情  
This matter needs to be solved quickly. ->> 这件事情需要迅速解决。  
Let's focus on the matter at hand. ->> 让我们专注于手头的事情。  

children /ˈʧɪldrən/ n. 孩子们（child的复数）  
The children are playing in the park. ->> 孩子们在公园里玩耍。  
She reads stories to the children every night. ->> 她每晚给孩子们讲故事。  

tired /taɪərd/ adj. 累,疲乏  
He felt tired after the long walk. ->> 长时间走路后他感到很累。  
She looks tired from studying all night. ->> 她熬夜学习后看起来很疲惫。  

boy /bɔɪ/ n. 男孩  
The boy is learning to ride a bike. ->> 男孩正在学骑自行车。  
That boy loves playing soccer. ->> 那个男孩喜欢踢足球。  

thirsty /ˈθərsti/ adj. 渴  
She was thirsty after running. ->> 她跑步后感到口渴。  
He drank water because he was thirsty. ->> 他因为口渴而喝水。  

Mum /məm/ n. 妈妈（儿语）  
Mum is cooking dinner in the kitchen. ->> 妈妈在厨房做晚饭。  
I gave Mum a hug before school. ->> 上学前我给了妈妈一个拥抱。  

sit /sɪt/ down 坐下  
Please sit down and relax. ->> 请坐下放松一下。  
He asked her to sit down and talk. ->> 他请她坐下来谈谈。  

right /raɪt/ adj. 好，可以  
You are right about the answer. ->> 你的答案是对的。  
It's right to help others in need. ->> 帮助有需要的人是正确的。  

ice /aɪs/ cream 冰淇淋  
She loves eating ice cream in summer. ->> 她喜欢在夏天吃冰淇淋。  
They bought ice cream for the party. ->> 他们为聚会买了冰淇淋。
