Late in the afternoon, the boys put up their tent in the middle of a field. ->> 傍晚时分，孩子们在田野中央搭起了帐篷。
As soon as this was done, they cooked a meal over an open fire. ->> 这件事刚做完，他们就在篝火上烧起了饭。
They were all hungry and the food smelled good. ->> 他们全都饿了，饭菜散发出阵阵香味。
After a wonderful meal, they told stories and sang songs by the campfire. ->> 他们美美地吃了一顿饭后，就围在营火旁讲起了故事，唱起了歌。
But some time later it began to rain. ->> 但过了一阵子，天下起雨来。
The boys felt tired so they put out the fire and crept into their tent. ->> 孩子们感到疲倦，于是扑灭了篝火，钻进了帐篷。
Their sleeping bags were warm and comfortable, so they all slept soundly. ->> 睡袋既暖和又舒服，所以，他们都睡得很香。
In the middle of the night, two boys woke up and began shouting. ->> 午夜时分，有两个孩子醒来，大声叫了起来。
The tent was full of water! ->> 原来帐篷里到处都是水！
They all leapt out of their sleeping bags and hurried outside. ->> 他们全都跳出睡袋，跑到外面。
It was raining heavily and they found that a stream had formed in the field. ->> 雨下得很大，他们发现田野里已经形成了一条小溪。
The stream wound its way across the field and then flowed right under their tent! ->> 那小溪弯弯曲曲穿过田野，然后正好从他们的帐篷底下流过去。
