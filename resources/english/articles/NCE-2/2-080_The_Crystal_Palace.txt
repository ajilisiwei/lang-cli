On arriving in England, they were taken to the Crystal Palace by train. ->> 一到英国，火车就把他们送到了水晶宫。
There were six million visitors in all, and the profits from the exhibition were used to build museums and colleges. ->> 参观的人数总共达到600万，博览会的利润被用来建造博物馆和高等学校。
