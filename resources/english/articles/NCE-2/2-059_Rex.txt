Our dog, Rex, used to sit outside our front gate and bark. ->> 我家的狗雷克斯，过去常坐在大门外面叫。
Every time he wanted to come into the garden he would bark until someone opened the gate. ->> 每当它想进花园时，便汪汪叫个不停，直到有人把门打开。
As the neighbours complained of the noise, my husband spent weeks training him to press his paw on the latch to let himself in. ->> 由于邻居们对噪音有意见，我丈夫花了几个星期的时间训练它用脚爪按住门闩自己进来。
Rex soon became an expert at opening the gate. ->> 雷克斯很快成了开门的专家。
However, when I was going out shopping last week, I noticed him in the garden near the gate. ->> 然而上星期我正要出去买东西时，看到它在花园里靠近大门的地方。
This time he was barking so that someone would let him out! ->> 这次它在叫着让人把它放出去！
Since then, he has developed another bad habit. ->> 从那以后，它养成了另外一种坏习惯。
As soon as he opens the gate from the outside, he comes into the garden and waits until the gate shuts. ->> 它从外面把门打开后，就走进花园，等着门关上。
Then he sits and barks until someone lets him out. ->> 然后它就坐下汪汪叫，直到有人把它放出去。
After this he immediately lets himself in and begins barking again. ->> 出去之后，它立刻又自己进来，然后再开始叫。
Yesterday my husband removed the gate and Rex got so annoyed we have not seen him since. ->> 昨天，我丈夫把门卸了下来，雷克斯很生气，此后我们再也没有见到它。


bark /bɑrk/ v. 狗叫
The bark of our neighbor's dog was so loud that it woke me up. ->> 我邻居的狗叫声太响了，把我吵醒了。
I knew something was wrong when I heard the bark late at night. ->> 当我深夜听到狗叫声时，我就知道出了什么事。

press /prɛs/ v. 按，压
You need to press the button firmly to start the machine. ->> 你需要用力按下按钮来启动机器。
Please press the dough gently to flatten it. ->> 请轻轻地按揉面团使其平整。

paw /pɔ/ n. 脚爪
The cat gently placed its paw on my hand. ->> 猫轻轻地把它的脚爪放在我手上。
After the hike, the dog's paw was covered in mud. ->> 徒步旅行后，狗的脚爪沾满了泥。

latch /læʧ/ n. 门闩
Make sure the latch is secure before you leave the house. ->> 离开房子前，要确保门闩是安全的。
The old barn door creaked as I lifted the latch. ->> 当我提起门闩时，旧谷仓的门嘎吱作响。

expert /ˈɛkspərt/ n. 专家
The expert was called in to give advice on the project. ->> 专家被请来对项目提供建议。
In that field, she's considered an expert due to her extensive research. ->> 在那个领域，由于她的广泛研究，她被认为是专家。

develop /dɪˈvɛləp/ v. 养成
It takes time to develop a new skill. ->> 养成一项新技能需要时间。
He managed to develop a taste for classical music over the years. ->> 多年来他养成了对古典音乐的品味。

habit /ˈhæbət/ n. 习惯
Drinking coffee every morning is a habit for many people. ->> 每天早晨喝咖啡是很多人的习惯。
She's trying to break the habit of biting her nails. ->> 她正在努力改掉咬指甲的习惯。

remove /riˈmuv/ v. 拆掉，取下
You need to remove your shoes before entering the house. ->> 进入房子前你需要脱鞋。
Carefully remove the sticker so that it doesn't tear. ->> 小心地把贴纸取下来，以免撕破。
