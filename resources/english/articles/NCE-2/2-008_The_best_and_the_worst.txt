Bill Frith's garden is larger than Joe's. ->> 比尔·弗里斯的花园比乔的花园大。
Bill works harder than Joe and grows more flowers and vegetables, but Joe's garden is more interesting. ->> 比尔比乔更为勤奋种植，并且种植的花卉和蔬菜更多，但乔的花园更富有情趣。
He has made neat paths and has built a wooden bridge over a pool. ->> 他修筑了整洁的小路，并在一个池塘上架了一座木桥。
I like gardens too, but I do not like hard work. ->> 我也喜欢花园，但我不愿意辛勤劳动。
Every year I enter for the garden competition too, and I always win a little prize for the worst garden in the town! ->> 每年我也参加花园竞赛，但总是因为拥有镇上最差的花园而获得一个小奖！

The competition draws participants from all over the world. ->> 比赛，竞赛吸引了来自世界各地的参与者。  
She won the competition last year and hopes to defend her title. ->> 她去年赢得了比赛，竞赛，希望能卫冕。

neat /nit/ adj. 整齐的，整洁的  
He dressed in a neat suit for the interview. ->> 他穿了一套整齐的，整洁的西装去面试。  
The librarian maintained a neat and organized collection of books. ->> 图书管理员维护了一个整齐的，整洁的书籍收藏。

path /pæθ/ n. 小路，小径  
The path through the forest led us to a beautiful clearing. ->> 穿过森林的小路，小径把我们带到了一个美丽的空地。  
They followed the path to the summit, enjoying the views along the way. ->> 他们沿着小路，小径攀登到山顶，沿途欣赏风景。

wooden /ˈwʊdən/ adj. 木头的  
The wooden bench under the tree was a perfect spot for a rest. ->> 树下的木头的长椅是休息的完美地方。  
She admired the intricate carvings on the wooden doorway. ->> 她欣赏着木头的门道上的复杂雕刻。

pool /pul/ n. 水池  
The pool is open for public swimming during the summer months. ->> 水池在夏季对公众开放游泳。  
The children splashed around in the pool all afternoon. ->> 孩子们整个下午都在水池里嬉戏。
