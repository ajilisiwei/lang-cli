Parents are often upset when their children praise the homes of their friends and regard it as a slur on their own cooking, or cleaning, or furniture, and often are foolish enough to let the adolescents see that they are annoyed. ->> 当家长听到孩子赞扬朋友的家时，总感到不安，认为这是对自己饭菜、卫生或家具的贬低，而且常常愚蠢地让青少年看出他们的烦恼。

They may even accuse them of disloyalty, or make some spiteful remark about the friends' parents. ->> 他们甚至可能指责孩子不忠，或者说一些朋友家长的坏话。

Such loss of dignity and descent into childish behaviour on the part to their parents about the place or people they visit. ->> 家长的这种失去尊严并表现出孩子气的行为，会让青少年感到震惊，并下定决心不再与父母分享他们访问过的地方或见过的人。

Before very long the parents will be complaining that the child is so secretive and never tells them anything, but they seldom realize that they have brought this on themselves. ->> 不久之后，家长就会抱怨孩子守口如瓶，什么也不告诉他们，但他们很少意识到这是自己造成的。

Disillusionment with the parents, however good and adequate they may be both as parents and as individuals, is to some degree inevitable. ->> 不管家长作为父母或个人有多么优秀合格，孩子对他们幻想的破灭在某种程度上是不可避免的。

Most children have such a high ideal of their parents, unless the parents themselves have been unsatisfactory, that it can hardly hope to stand up to a realistic evaluation. ->> 除非父母本身不令人满意，否则大多数孩子对父母的评价过高，以致这种评价很难经受住现实的考验。

Parents would be greatly surprised and deeply touched if they realized how much belief their children usually have in their character and infallibility, and how much this faith means to a child. ->> 如果家长意识到孩子们通常是多么相信他们的品行和绝对正确，以及这种信念对孩子意味着什么，那么他们会大感惊讶和感动。

If parents were prepared for this adolescent reaction, and realized that it was a sign that the child was growing up and developing valuable powers of observation and independent judgment, they would not be so hurt, and therefore would not drive the child into opposition by resenting and resisting it. ->> 如果家长对这种青少年的反应有所准备，并意识到这表明孩子正在成长并发展宝贵的观察力和独立判断力，那么他们就不会那么受伤，从而不会因为怨恨和抵触这种反应而把孩子推到对立面。

The adolescent, with his passion for sincerity, always respects a parent who admits that he is wrong, or ignorant, or even that he has been unfair or unjust. ->> 青少年非常热爱坦诚，对于能够承认错误、无知，甚至自己曾经不公平或不公正的父母，他们总是心存敬意。

What the child cannot forgive is the parent's refusal to admit these charges if the child knows them to be true. ->> 孩子们无法原谅的是：如果他们知道这些指责是事实，而父母却拒绝承认。

Victorian parents believed that they kept their dignity by retreating behind an unreasoning authoritarian attitude; in fact they did nothing of the kind, but children were then too cowed to let them know how they really felt. ->> 维多利亚时代的父母认为自己能以不讲道理的权威态度维持尊严，实际上他们并未做到这一点，但那时的孩子因为害怕而不敢让父母知道自己的真实感受。

Today we tend to go to the other extreme, but on the whole this is a healthier attitude both for the child and the parent. ->> 如今我们倾向于走向另一个极端，但总的来说，这对父母和孩子而言是一种更健康的态度。

It is always wiser and safer to face up to reality, however painful it may be at the moment. ->> 遇事采取面对现实的态度总是更明智、更稳妥，尽管当下可能会很痛苦。
