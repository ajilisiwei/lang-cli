We keep trying all four methods, but the sheer volume of rubbish we produce worldwide threatens to overwhelm us. ->> 我们一直在尝试这四种方法，但我们在全球范围内产生的垃圾量巨大，几乎要把我们掩埋。

The need to produce ever-increasing quantities of cheap food leads to a different kind of pollution. ->> 日益增长的对廉价食物的需求导致了另一种污染。
Industrialized farming methods produce cheap meat products: beef, pork and chicken. ->> 工业化的耕作方式生产出廉价的肉类产品：牛肉、猪肉和鸡肉。
The use of pesticides and fertilizers produces cheap grain and vegetables. ->> 使用杀虫剂和化肥生产出廉价的谷物和蔬菜。
