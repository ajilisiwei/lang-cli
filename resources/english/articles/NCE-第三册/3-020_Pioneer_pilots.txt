In 1908 Lord Northcliffe offered a prize of $1,000 to the first man who would fly across the English Channel. ->> 1908年，诺斯克利夫勋爵拿出1,000美元，作为对第一个飞越英吉利海峡的人的奖励。
Over a year passed before the first attempt was made. ->> 一年多过去了才有人首次尝试。
On July 19th, 1909, in the early morning, Hubert Latham took off from the French coast in his plane the 'Antoinette IV.' He had travelled only seven miles across the Channel when his engine failed and he was forced to land on sea. ->> 1909年7月19日凌晨，休伯特·莱瑟姆驾驶“安托瓦内特四号”飞机从法国海岸起飞，但飞越海峡仅7英里后引擎故障，被迫降落在海面上。
The 'Antoinette' floated on the water until Latham was picked up by a ship. ->> “安托瓦内特”号飞机在海上漂浮，直到莱瑟姆被一艘船救起。
Two days later, Louis Bleriot arrived near Calais with a plane called 'No. XI'. ->> 两天之后，路易斯·布莱里奥驾驶一架名为“11号”的飞机来到加来附近。
Bleriot had been making planes since 1905 and this was his latest model. ->> 布莱里奥从1905年起便开始研制飞机，“11号”是他制作的最新型号。
A week before, he had completed a successful overland flight during which he covered twenty-six miles. ->> 一周以前，他成功完成了一次26英里的陆上飞行。
Latham, however, did not give up easily. ->> 然而，莱瑟姆并不轻易放弃。
He, too, arrived near Calais on the same day with a new 'Antoinette'. ->> 同一天，他驾驶一架新的“安托瓦内特”号飞机也来到了加来附近。
It looked as if there would be an exciting race across the Channel. ->> 看来将会有一场激烈的英吉利海峡飞越竞赛。
Both planes were going to take off on July 25th, but Latham failed to get up early enough. After making a short test flight at 4.15 a.m., Bleriot set off half an hour later. ->> 两架飞机都打算在7月25日起飞，但莱瑟姆起床太晚。布莱里奥在凌晨4点15分进行了一次短距离试飞，半小时后便正式出发。
His great flight lasted thirty-seven minutes. ->> 他这次伟大的飞行持续了37分钟。
When he landed near Dover, the first person to greet him was a local policeman. ->> 当他在多佛着陆后，第一个迎接他的是当地的一名警察。
Latham made another attempt a week later and got within half a mile of Dover, but he was unlucky again. ->> 莱瑟姆一周后再次尝试，飞到离多佛不到半英里的地方，但又一次不走运。
His engine failed and he landed on the sea for the second time. ->> 引擎再次故障，他第二次降落在海面上。
