Though a little suspicious this time, the policeman gave him the same answer. ->> 虽然这次有些怀疑，但警察还是给了他同样的答复。
The day after, Patrick went to the house once more and asked exactly the same question. ->> 第三天，帕特里克再一次去了那所房子，问了完全同样的问题。
This time, the policeman lost his temper. ->> 这一次，警察火了。
