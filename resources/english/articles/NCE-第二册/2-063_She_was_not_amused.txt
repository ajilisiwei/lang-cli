circle /ˈsərkəl/ n. 圈子  
The circle of friends she built in college stayed with her for life. ->> 她在大学里建立的朋友圈子伴随了她一生。  
He managed to join an exclusive circle of entrepreneurs in the city. ->> 他设法加入了城市里一个独特的企业家圈子。  

admire /ædˈmaɪr/ v. 赞美，钦佩  
I truly admire her dedication and hard work. ->> 我真心钦佩她的奉献精神和努力工作。  
They admire the architecture of the old cathedral. ->> 他们钦佩那座老教堂的建筑。  

close /kloʊz/ adj. 亲密的  
They have been close friends since childhood. ->> 他们自孩提时代起就是亲密的朋友。  
The siblings have always shared a close bond. ->> 兄弟姐妹之间一直有着亲密的关系。  

wedding /ˈwɛdɪŋ/ n. 婚礼  
Their wedding in the countryside was both intimate and beautiful. ->> 他们在乡村的婚礼既亲密又美丽。  
The couple planned a small wedding with just family and close friends. ->> 这对夫妇计划举行一个只有家人和亲密朋友的小型婚礼。

The company hosted a lavish reception to celebrate its anniversary. ->> 公司举办了一场盛大的招待会以庆祝其周年纪念。  
We attended a formal reception at the ambassador's residence. ->> 我们参加了在大使官邸举行的正式招待会。  

sort /sɔrt/ n. 种类  
There is a sort of clarity that comes with quiet reflection. ->> 经过安静的反思得到一种清晰。
