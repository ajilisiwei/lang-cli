If she comes, she will get a surprise. ->> 如果她来了，她会感到非常惊讶。

We are now living in a beautiful new house in the country. ->> 我们现在住在乡间的一栋漂亮的新住宅里。

Work on it had begun before my sister left. ->> 在我姐姐离开之前，这栋房子的建设就已开始。

The house was completed five months ago. ->> 房子在5个月以前竣工。

In my letter, I told her that she could stay with us. ->> 我在信中告诉她，她可以和我们住在一起。

The house has many large rooms and there is a lovely garden. ->> 这栋房子里有许多房间，还有一个漂亮的花园。

It is a very modern house, so it looks strange to some people. ->> 它是一栋非常现代化的住宅，因此在有些人看来很古怪。

It must be the only modern house in the district. ->> 它肯定是这个地区唯一的一栋现代化住宅。


complete /kəmˈplit/ v. 完成  
He managed to complete the project on time. ->> 他设法按时完成了项目。  
After several months, they finally complete the training program. ->> 几个月后，他们终于完成了培训计划。

modern /ˈmɑdərn/ adj. 新式的，与以往不同的  
The modern design of the building attracted many architecture enthusiasts. ->> 这座建筑的新式设计吸引了许多建筑爱好者。  
She prefers modern art over classical paintings. ->> 她更喜欢新式艺术而不是古典绘画。

strange /streɪnʤ/ adj. 奇怪的  
I found it strange that the store was closed during normal hours. ->> 我发现商店在正常营业时间关闭是件怪事。  
The strange sound in the night kept me awake for hours. ->> 夜晚的奇怪声音让我醒了好几个小时。

district /ˈdɪstrɪkt/ n. 地区  
The city decided to invest in developing the business district. ->> 这座城市决定投资开发商业区。  
We explored the historic district during our vacation. ->> 我们在度假期间探索了历史地区。
