Meanwhile, my tongue was busy searching out the hole where the tooth had been. ->> 与此同时，我的舌头正在忙着寻找刚拔掉的那颗牙的伤口。
I suddenly felt very worried, but could not say anything. ->> 我突然非常着急起来，但却什么也说不出来。
When the dentist at last removed the cotton wool from my mouth, I was able to tell him that he had pulled out the wrong tooth. ->> 当那位牙医最终将药棉从我嘴中取出时，我终于能够告诉他，他拔错了牙。
