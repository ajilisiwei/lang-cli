The rare species was spotted in the remote forest. ->> 这种罕见的物种在偏远的森林里被发现。  
Her talent is rare and truly exceptional. ->> 她的才华罕见且真正出众。  
  
ancient /ˈeɪnʧənt/ adj. 古代的，古老的  
The ancient ruins attracted historians from around the world. ->> 这些古代的遗迹吸引了来自世界各地的历史学家。  
She felt a connection to the ancient traditions during the festival. ->> 在节日期间，她感受到与古老传统的联系。

The myth of the lost city has intrigued explorers for centuries. ->> 失落之城的神话故事几个世纪以来一直吸引着探险家。  
He enjoys reading about Greek myths and legends. ->> 他喜欢阅读关于希腊神话和传说的书籍。  
  
trouble /ˈtrəbəl/ n. 麻烦  
He found himself in trouble when the car broke down. ->> 当汽车抛锚时，他发现自己遇上了麻烦。  
The trouble with this approach is that it's too complex. ->> 这种方法的麻烦在于它过于复杂。  
  
effect /ˈifɛkt/ n. 结果，效果  
The new policy had an immediate effect on reducing pollution. ->> 新政策对减少污染产生了直接的效果。  
Witnessing the sunrise had a calming effect on her. ->> 观看日出对她产生了平静的效果。  
  
Medusa /məˈdusə/ n. 美杜莎（古希腊神话中3位蛇发女怪之一）  
The legend of Medusa has inspired many artists through the ages. ->> 美杜莎的传说激励了许多艺术家。  
Medusa's gaze is a powerful symbol in literature and art. ->> 美杜莎的凝视在文学和艺术中是一个强大的象征。

The story of the Gorgon warned people about vanity and pride. ->> 关于蛇发女怪的故事警示人们关于虚荣和骄傲。  
In mythology, the Gorgon was feared for its petrifying gaze. ->> 在神话中，蛇发女怪因其石化的凝视而令人畏惧。
