My aunt Jennifer is an actress. ->> 我的姑姑詹妮弗是位演员。
She must be at least thirty-five years old. ->> 她至少有35岁了。
In spite of this, she often appears on the stage as a young girl. ->> 尽管如此，她却常在舞台上扮演小姑娘。
Jennifer will have to take part in a new play soon. ->> 詹妮弗很快又要参加一个新剧的演出。
This time, she will be a girl of seventeen. ->> 这一次，她将扮演一个17岁的少女。
In the play, she must appear in a bright red dress and long black stockings. ->> 演出时她必须穿一条鲜红色的裙子和黑色的长筒袜。
Last year in another play, she had to wear short socks and a bright, orange-coloured dress. ->> 去年在演另一个剧时，她不得不穿短袜和一件鲜艳的橘红色的衣服。
If anyone ever asks her how old she is, she always answers, 'Darling, it must be terrible to be grown up!' ->> 一旦有人问起她有多大年纪，她总是回答：“亲爱的，长成大人真可怕啊！”


appear /əˈpɪr/ v. 登场，扮演
Her role will appear in the final scene of the play. ->> 她的角色将在该剧的最后一幕登场。
He was excited to appear in his first major film this summer. ->> 他非常兴奋今年夏天能在他的第一部主要电影中登场。

stage /steɪʤ/ n. 舞台
The actor walked confidently onto the stage. ->> 演员自信地走上舞台。
The stage was set for the evening performance. ->> 舞台为晚上的演出设好了布景。

bright /braɪt/ adj. 鲜艳的
She wore a bright dress that caught everyone's attention. ->> 她穿着一件鲜艳的连衣裙，引起了所有人的注意。
The bright sunshine lifted everyone's spirits. ->> 明媚的阳光让每个人的精神都振奋了起来。

stocking /ˈstɑkɪŋ/ n. （女用）长筒袜
She carefully chose a patterned stocking to match her dress. ->> 她精心挑选了一双图案长筒袜来搭配她的连衣裙。
The old theater displayed a collection of vintage stockings. ->> 这家古老的剧院展出了几款复古长筒袜。

sock /sɑk/ n. 短袜
He put on a fresh pair of socks before heading out for his jog. ->> 在出去慢跑之前，他换上了一双新短袜。
The bright colors of the children's socks were hard to miss. ->> 孩子们袜子的鲜艳颜色很难被忽视。
